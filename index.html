Opportunity Score /100 (ranking basis): 30% TradingView technical rating + 15% trend & momentum (price vs 50-day average, 1-month performance) + 15% valuation (P/E vs the whole market, cheaper = higher) + 10% dividend yield + 15% RSI positioning (sweet spot 40–65: room to rise, not overheated) + 15% liquidity. A blended score keeps the ranking useful in all market conditions — pure momentum tops out in rallies, pure value tops out in sell-offs. Allocation: dynamic — proportional to the cube of each stock's Opportunity Score across the top 5 (a clearly stronger setup earns a larger share; near-equal scores split near-equally), capped at 50% per name, floored at 3%, total 100%. EMA Δ (21): the latest-day difference (SAR) between (a) the 21-day EMA of daily close prices and (b) the EMA of that EMA. Both use daily data as of the last session. Positive = uptrend, negative = downtrend. Hover for both EMA values. (a) is exact from TradingView; (b) approximated by lag equivalence (≈ EMA41, interpolated from EMA40/EMA50) since no source publishes double-smoothed series.
